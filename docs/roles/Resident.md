# Resident Guide

## Main Features
- View collection schedules
- Submit issue reports
- Access IEC materials
- Notifications and feedback
- Settings

## Common Tasks
1. Check your collection day/time
2. Report missed collection or bin issues
3. Read IEC guides on segregation and schedules
4. Send feedback or suggestions

## Tips
- Keep your address and barangay updated in Settings.
- Turn on notifications for schedule changes.
