Hostinger Cron Job commands (Asia/Manila)

Replace:
- YOUR_DOMAIN with your domain (e.g., example.com)
- YOUR_SECRET with your secret key (same as in broadcast_timein_notifications.php)
- If the project is under a subfolder, keep /kolektrash in the URL.

5:00 AM – open window
/usr/bin/curl -s https://YOUR_DOMAIN/kolektrash/backend/api/broadcast_timein_notifications.php?type=open\&key=YOUR_SECRET

5:55 AM – near deadline warning
/usr/bin/curl -s https://YOUR_DOMAIN/kolektrash/backend/api/broadcast_timein_notifications.php?type=warning\&key=YOUR_SECRET

6:00 AM – closed
/usr/bin/curl -s https://YOUR_DOMAIN/kolektrash/backend/api/broadcast_timein_notifications.php?type=closed\&key=YOUR_SECRET

Note: Ensure your site timezone is set to Asia/Manila in PHP or Hostinger hPanel.
